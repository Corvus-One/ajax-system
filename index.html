🗓️ Weekly Rhythm (Mon–Thu)

  1. Monday (30 min): Open Agents tab → run Agent 02 (Job Scout). Review 10–15 scored roles. Flag your picks.
  2. Mon–Thu (20 min/day): For each flagged role — run Agent 04 (Resume Tailor), paste the JD, get a tailored resume. Then build outreach in the Outreach tab. Apply and log in Pipeline.
  3. Every 48 hrs: Agent 03 (Email Watch) runs in a separate Claude chat with Gmail connected — it's the one exception.
  4. Before every interview: Agent 05 (Interview Prep) — paste the JD, select interview type, get 20 Q&A. Practice out loud.
  5. Thursday (15 min): Agent 07 (Weekly Review) — paste your pipeline stats, get specific adjustments for next week.

🔐 Clearance — Get In The Door Strategy

  • Target "preferred, not required" roles. Sponsors exist — you just have to ask.
  • Lead with eligibility. "I'm eligible with no barriers — clean background, US citizen, willing to undergo SF-86."
  • First recruiter call question: "Is there budget to sponsor clearance for the right candidate?"
  • Clearance builds with tenure. Secret: 3–6 months. TS: 12–18 months. Get in first.
  • OCONUS roles often sponsor faster due to mission urgency.

💰 Salary Negotiation Rules

  • Never give a number first. Ask for the budgeted range instead.
  • Your anchor = floor + 15%. Open there, let them counter up.
  • Total comp matters. Bonus, equity, clearance sponsorship ($15K–$30K value), training budget.
  • Get it in writing before verbal acceptance. Always.
